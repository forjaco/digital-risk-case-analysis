# Fraud Indicators Playbook

This playbook documents common indicators observed in online fraud and digital abuse operations, supporting faster threat identification.

---

## Fake Online Store Indicators
- Extremely low prices compared to market value
- Recently created domains
- No verifiable company information
- Limited or fake contact channels
- Payment requests via instant transfer only
- No clear refund or return policies

---

## Phishing Indicators
- Urgent messages requesting immediate action
- Login pages mimicking banks or services
- Suspicious sender domains
- Links redirecting to unknown domains
- Requests for credentials or financial information

---

## Brand Impersonation Indicators
- Domains visually similar to legitimate brands
- Fake promotions or support channels
- Copy of brand logos and visual identity
- Social media profiles imitating companies

---

## Marketplace Fraud Indicators
- Sellers requesting payment outside platforms
- Fake payment confirmation messages
- Delivery promises without tracking information
- Newly created seller accounts

---

## Analyst Notes
Indicators evolve as attackers change techniques; continuous updates are recommended.
