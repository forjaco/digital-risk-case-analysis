# Threat Classification Model

This model supports standardized classification of digital threats.

---

## Threat Categories

### Phishing
Fraud campaigns designed to steal credentials or financial information.

### Fraud Operations
Fake services or stores aiming to obtain payments illegally.

### Brand Impersonation
Unauthorized use of brand identity to deceive users.

### Account Impersonation
Fake social media or platform accounts posing as legitimate entities.

### Malware Distribution
Distribution of malicious software through deceptive channels.

---

## Severity Levels

### Low
Limited impact or low exposure risk.

### Medium
Moderate exposure risk affecting a small group of users.

### High
Significant risk of financial or data loss.

### Critical
Large-scale threat affecting many users or organizations.

---

## Analyst Notes
Consistent classification improves incident prioritization and response efficiency.
