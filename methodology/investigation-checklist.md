# Digital Risk Investigation Checklist

This checklist is used to support the initial evaluation of suspected digital risk cases, including phishing, fraud, and brand impersonation scenarios.

---

## 1. Initial Case Review
- [ ] Identify reported URL, domain, or platform content
- [ ] Determine if the content mimics a legitimate brand or service
- [ ] Check for suspicious promotions or urgent messaging
- [ ] Verify presence of misleading contact or payment information

---

## 2. Domain and Infrastructure Review
- [ ] Observe suspicious domain naming patterns
- [ ] Verify if domain appears recently created
- [ ] Identify unusual hosting infrastructure patterns
- [ ] Check for multiple similar domains or cloned structures
