# Takedown Workflow Model

This workflow represents a standard digital abuse takedown process.

---

## Step 1 – Case Identification
Threat reported or detected through monitoring systems.

---

## Step 2 – Investigation
Analyst reviews infrastructure, content, and risk indicators.

---

## Step 3 – Risk Classification
Threat severity and potential impact are determined.

---

## Step 4 – Notification
Appropriate entities are notified:
- Hosting provider
- Domain registrar
- Platform abuse teams
- Affected organizations

---

## Step 5 – Takedown Monitoring
Case monitored until content removal or mitigation.

---

## Step 6 – Documentation
Case recorded for future reference and pattern analysis.

---

## Analyst Notes
Workflow efficiency directly impacts victim exposure reduction.
