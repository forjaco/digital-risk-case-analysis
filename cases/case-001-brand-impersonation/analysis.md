## Case Overview
A website was identified impersonating a retail brand, attempting to mislead users through visual identity replication.

## Identification
Indicators observed:
- Domain visually similar to official brand domain
- Recently registered domain
- Hosting provider commonly abused in phishing operations
- Fake promotional or login interface

## Risk Assessment
Risk level: High

Potential impacts:
- Credential theft
- Financial fraud
- Brand reputation damage
- User data exposure

## Recommended Actions
- Notify hosting provider
- Notify domain registrar
- Notify brand abuse team

## Key Indicators
Domain: example-support-login.com
Hosting: Unknown
Threat type: Brand impersonation

## Analyst Notes
This scenario demonstrates how attackers leverage brand trust to exploit users through deceptive infrastructure.
