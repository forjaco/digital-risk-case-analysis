## Case Overview
A fraudulent online store was identified offering products at highly discounted prices to attract victims. The website mimicked legitimate e-commerce behavior but was created solely to collect payments without delivering products.

## Identification
Indicators observed:
- Recently registered domain
- Excessive discounts inconsistent with market prices
- Lack of verifiable company information
- Absence of customer support channels
- Suspicious payment methods requesting instant transfers

## Attack Technique
Attackers created a fake e-commerce platform using copied product images and promotional content. Victims are redirected via social media advertisements or messaging platforms and persuaded to make payments through instant transfer methods.

## Risk Assessment
Risk level: High

Potential impacts:
- Financial loss for victims
- Abuse of consumer trust
- Brand damage if legitimate brands are impersonated
- Increased fraud campaign replicati
