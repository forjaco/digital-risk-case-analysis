## Case Overview
A phishing campaign targeting banking customers was identified using deceptive messages directing victims to fraudulent login pages designed to steal credentials and financial information.

## Identification
Indicators observed:
- Fake banking login interface
- Suspicious domain mimicking legitimate bank domains
- Urgent messaging requesting account verification
- Infrastructure hosted outside official banking networks

## Attack Technique
Attackers distribute phishing messages via email or messaging platforms, creating urgency related to account security or transaction verification. Victims are redirected to fraudulent portals capturing credentials.

## Risk Assessment
Risk level: Critical

Potential impacts:
- Credential theft
- Account takeover
- Financial fraud
- Personal data exposure

## Recommended Actions
- Notify hosting provider
- Report domain to registrar
- Notify financial institution abuse team
- Monitor emergence of similar domains

## Key Indicators
Threat type: Banking phishing
Objective: Credential harvesting and financial fraud

## Analyst Conclusion
Banking phishing campaigns remain effective due to urgency tactics and trust exploitation. Rapid detection and takedown operations are essential to minimize user exposure.
