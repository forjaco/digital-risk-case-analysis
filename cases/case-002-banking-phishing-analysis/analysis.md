## Case Overview
A phishing campaign targeting bank customers was identified using deceptive messages and fraudulent websites designed to steal user credentials.

## Identification
Indicators observed:
- Fake banking login interface
- Suspicious domain structure
- Urgent messaging requesting account verification
- Infrastructure hosted outside official banking networks

## Attack Technique
Attackers used social engineering tactics to create urgency, directing victims to fraudulent login pages mimicking official banking portals.

## Risk Assessment
Risk level: Critical

Potential impacts:
- Credential theft
- Financial fraud
- Account takeover
- Personal data exposure

## Recommended Actions
- Notify hosting provider
- Report domain to registrar
- Notify affected institution
- Monitor similar phishing domains

## Key Indicators
Threat type: Banking phishing
Objective: Credential harvesting and financial fraud

## Analyst Conclusion
Banking phishing campaigns remain highly effective due to urgency tactics and user trust in financial institutions. Continuous monitoring and rapid takedown operations are critical to reduce impact.
