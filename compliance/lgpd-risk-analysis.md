## Overview
This document analyzes how digital threats such as phishing, fraud, and online impersonation can lead to personal data exposure under LGPD regulations.

## Common Risk Scenarios
- Phishing attacks resulting in credential theft
- Fake websites collecting personal data
- Fraudulent platforms requesting personal information
- Social engineering attacks targeting employees

## Potential Impacts
- Personal data exposure
- Financial loss for users
- Reputational damage for companies
- Regulatory penalties

## Risk Mitigation Measures
- Employee awareness programs
- Access control policies
- Incident response procedures
- Continuous monitoring of online abuse

## Conclusion
Digital risk monitoring and takedown operations contribute significantly to reducing risks associated with personal data exposure and regulatory non-compliance.
